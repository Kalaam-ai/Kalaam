How we use Cookies

We use cookies to make Kalaam.ai work better for you. Here's exactly what we set, why, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our platform, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for core platform functionality such as authentication and session management. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., pages visited, session duration). We use anonymised data only.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as language preference and dashboard layout.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant content and measure campaign effectiveness. These are only set with your explicit consent.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from trusted third parties including Google Analytics for usage analytics and Stripe for payment processing. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing their use of cookie data.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Kalaam.ai platform. You can also opt out of analytics cookies at any time via our cookie preference centre, accessible from any page footer.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be communicated via a notice on our website. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
