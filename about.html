Who We Are

Building the future of Speech

We're a team of engineers, linguists, and business operators on a mission to eliminate the language barrier between businesses and their customers.

Our Story

Built for businesses that speak two worlds

Kalaam.ai was founded in 2023 by a team of engineers and linguists who noticed a glaring gap: businesses across Pakistan and the Gulf were losing customers every day because their phone systems only spoke one language — and it wasn't always the customer's.

The word Kalaam (کلام) means "speech" or "discourse" in Urdu and Arabic — the art of meaningful communication. That's what we set out to build: AI that doesn't just answer calls, but genuinely communicates.

Today, Kalaam.ai powers thousands of business conversations daily across healthcare, real estate, logistics, and financial services — in English and Urdu, 24 hours a day.

Our Mission

To eliminate the language barrier between businesses and their customers by building the most natural, intelligent bilingual voice AI in the world.

We believe every customer deserves to be heard in their own language — and every business deserves a phone system that never sleeps, never misses a call, and always represents them professionally.
