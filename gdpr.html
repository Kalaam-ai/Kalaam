Your data rights Under GDPR

Kalaam.ai is fully committed to GDPR compliance. This page explains how we handle EU and UK personal data and how you can exercise your rights.

Our Commitment to GDPR

Kalaam.ai is committed to full compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR. This page explains how we handle the data of individuals in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom.

Data Controller & Processor

When you use Kalaam.ai's services, your business acts as the Data Controller for call data involving your customers. Kalaam.ai acts as a Data Processor, handling that data according to your documented instructions and our Data Processing Agreement (DPA).

To request a copy of our DPA, contact privacy@kalaam.ai.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing your account data to deliver the services you've subscribed to.
  • Legitimate interests: Analytics and security monitoring to improve and protect our platform.
  • Consent: Marketing communications and non-essential cookies, where you have given explicit opt-in consent.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

Your GDPR Rights

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restriction (Art. 18): Request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

International Data Transfers

When we transfer personal data outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and adequacy decisions where applicable.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Call data defaults to a 90-day retention period; extended periods may be agreed contractually.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of your GDPR rights, submit a request to privacy@kalaam.ai. We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours and respond fully within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority.

Contact & DPO

Data Protection inquiries: privacy@kalaam.ai | Our Data Protection Officer can be reached at the same address.

Questions about your data?

Our team is happy to answer any privacy or compliance questions.
