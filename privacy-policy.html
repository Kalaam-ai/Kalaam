Your privacy is our Priority

We believe in transparent, responsible handling of your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kalaam.ai ('we', 'us', 'our') collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our AI voice platform and related services. By using Kalaam.ai, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last updated: March 2026. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Call Data: Audio recordings, transcripts, and metadata from calls processed by our AI system.
  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, phone number, and billing details provided during sign-up.
  • Usage Data: Log files, feature usage patterns, dashboard interactions, and API call records.
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and session identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our AI voice services.
  • Process transactions and send billing-related communications.
  • Train and refine our language models (using anonymised, aggregated data only — never individual identifiable recordings without explicit consent).
  • Send operational communications such as service updates and security alerts.
  • Respond to customer support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and conduct annual third-party security audits.

Call recordings and transcripts are stored for a default period of 90 days unless a longer retention period has been agreed in your service contract. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Trusted sub-processors who assist in providing our services (e.g., cloud infrastructure, payment processors), each bound by strict data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law.
  • Successor entities in the event of a merger or acquisition, with advance notice to affected customers.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing activities. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@kalaam.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@kalaam.ai | Kalaam.ai, Lahore, Pakistan & Dubai, UAE.

Questions about your data?

Our team is happy to answer any privacy or compliance questions.
